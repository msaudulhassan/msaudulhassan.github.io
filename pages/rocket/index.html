Target Seeking Rocket

[pdf]

Jacob Koos, Boluwatife Olabiran; Muhammad Saud Ul Hassan; Brian Van Stratum

Developed rocket kinematics and implementd a closed-loop feedback control algorithm in MATLAB/Simulink to dynamically correct the heading angle of a rocket in pursuit of a target.
