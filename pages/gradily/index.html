Gradily

Muhammad Saud Ul Hassan

Gradily is social media platform to connect prospective students applying abroad, especially those in underprivileged areas with limited access to educational consultants and quality study-abroad resources.

Gradily was short-listed among 130 best startups by Plan9 -- Pakistan's largest & most prestigious tech incubator. However, the work was put on hold when I started grad school.
