Muhammad Saud Ul Hassan
Senior Software Engineer @ AMD · Visiting Researcher @ Rice University

I am a senior software engineer at Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) working on MIOpen, AMD's open-source library for high-performance machine learning kernels. My role primarily concerns the development and integration of deep learning heuristics for kernel selection and tuning in MIOpen. Concurrently, I am a visiting researcher with Dr. Laura Schaefer at Rice University, working on the application of deep learning for sustainable energy solutions.

Previously, I was a research assistant with Dr. Ghulam Rasool at Rowan University, investigating performance degradation and mispredictions in bayesian neural networks. I have also been a research assistant with Dr. Christian Hubicki at Florida State University, where I worked on open-loop stable walking in bipedal robots using trajectory optimization.

I earned my master's degree in mechanical engineering (robotics) from Florida State University in 2021.
